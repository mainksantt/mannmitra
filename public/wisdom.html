भगवद्गीता के बारे में

The Bhagavad Gita is a 700-verse Hindu scripture that forms part of the Mahabharata. It presents a synthesis of Hindu ideas about dharma, devotion, and liberation. Spoken by Krishna to the warrior Arjuna, its wisdom applies to every aspect of life.

🚀

Naval Ravikant के बारे में

Naval Ravikant is an Indian-American entrepreneur, investor, and philosopher. His ideas on wealth creation, happiness as a skill, and specific knowledge have inspired millions of people around the world to rethink how they live and work.

🧠

Mental Wellness & Wisdom

Ancient and modern wisdom complement each other beautifully. The Gita's message of equanimity and Naval's emphasis on happiness as a skill together provide a practical framework for a meaningful, balanced life.
